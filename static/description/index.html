Procurement Management

Odoo 17 Community Edition

Algerian Public Procurement Management System
Compliant with Law n23-12 and Executive Decree 15-247

Overview

Complete solution for managing Algerian public procurement procedures including consultations, tenders, and competitions.

Key Features

  • Full procurement lifecycle management (draft to award)
  • Committee and session management
  • Bidder registration and evaluation
  • Envelope opening workflow
  • Technical and financial evaluation scoring
  • Award decision and notification
  • Service orders and contract management
  • Document management and verification
  • Multi-currency support
  • Comprehensive reporting

Procedure Types Supported

  • Open National Tender
  • Open International Tender
  • Restricted Tender
  • Consultation
  • Competition
  • Mutual Agreement

Technical Requirements

  • Odoo 17 Community Edition
  • Python 3.10+
  • PostgreSQL 14+

Dependencies

  • base
  • mail
  • purchase
  • contacts
  • website (optional)
